This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal data when you use the GokGokView mobile app and related services.

Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information to provide and improve the Service:

  • Account & Profile: name, email address, phone number, role (owner or tenant), profile photo, property identifiers (unit number, address), and other registration information.
  • Lease & Property Data: lease start/end dates, rent amount, payment history, maintenance requests, notes and messages between users, and other tenancy details you or your property manager provide.
  • Payment Information: if you make payments through the app, billing and transaction data (we do not store full card numbers — payment processing is handled by third-party provider(s) described below).
  • Device & Usage Data: device model, OS version, app version, crash reports, usage analytics (pages visited, features used), and IP address.
  • Photos & Attachments: images and documents you upload (e.g., photos for maintenance requests).
  • Communications: messages you send or receive through the app and support communications.

How we use information

  • To create and manage your account and provide the app functionality (leasing, payments, messaging, maintenance tracking).
  • To process transactions and send receipts and billing notices.
  • To communicate with you (support, account notices, security alerts).
  • To analyze usage and improve the app (analytics, crash reporting).
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse.
